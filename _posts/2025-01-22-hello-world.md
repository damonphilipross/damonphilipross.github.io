## How to write posts

Create a new markdown file in the `_posts` folder with the format:

```
YYYY-MM-DD-your-post-title.md
```

Add frontmatter at the top:

```yaml
---
layout: post
title: Your Post Title
date: 2025-01-22
---
```

Then write your content in markdown below.
